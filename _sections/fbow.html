For Better or Worse

Fbow is the final project of my master in programming and development of video games. It's a fully collaborative game, the result of 4 months of full-time teamwork. Feel free to play it and send your feedback!



Mia and Raka, renowned rogues, cannot stand each other. Connected by a magical chain, the two rivals are forced to cooperate if they want to be freed from their ethereal link. Send the Orb of Power back to each other to prevail over the enemies chasing you! Combine strength and agility and get new skills to travel across various locations in a colorful world! For Better or Worse - A unique, crazy and lively experience in a world combining animals and medieval fantasy!
